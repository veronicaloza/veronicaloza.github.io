Glow Girl Boutique Brand Identity

Spring 2025



Full Case Study
Glow Girl Boutique is a small clothing boutique located in the heart of Bali, that emphasizes gifting women's confidence, their inner-glow and love from their clothes. They reached out to rebrand their identity and their merchandise at Design Wonderland Branding Spring 2026 Course.

This is a case study of my version for their rebrand that won second place in the Design Wonderland Rebrand Competition among 300 participants.
