Glow Girl Boutque Brand Case Study

Glow Girl Boutique Website

Branding
Glow Girl Boutque is a small clothing boutique located in the heart of bali, that emphasizes on gifting woman's confidence, its inner-glow and love from their clothes. They reached out to create a rebranding of their logo, physical merchandising (shopping bags, thank you cards, clothing tags, etc.) and an instagramn mockup. This is a case study of my version for their rebrand that won second place in the Design Wonderland Rebrand Competition.
